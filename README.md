# 📊 GlobalMart Sales Analytics Dashboard

### Transforming Retail Sales Data into Actionable Business Insights with Microsoft Power BI

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The **GlobalMart Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights.

The project analyses historical sales transactions to identify trends in revenue, profitability, customer behaviour, product performance, and regional sales. By applying interactive dashboards and key performance indicators (KPIs), the solution enables decision-makers to monitor business performance and make informed, data-driven decisions.

Following the **Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining (CRISP-DM)** methodology, this project demonstrates the complete analytics lifecycle—from understanding the business problem and preparing data to creating interactive dashboards and communicating actionable recommendations.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

Retail organisations generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, without proper analysis, valuable business opportunities often remain hidden.

GlobalMart required a centralised analytical solution that would allow business stakeholders to:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Evaluate profitability across products and regions
* Understand customer purchasing behaviour
* Identify high-performing products and locations
* Support strategic business decisions using data

The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into interactive dashboards capable of providing meaningful insights for executives, sales managers, and business analysts.

# 🔄 Project Methodology (CRISP-DM)

This project followed the **Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining (CRISP-DM)** framework to ensure a structured and business-focused approach to analysing GlobalMart's retail sales data.

## 1. Business Understanding

The primary objective was to develop an interactive dashboard that enables business stakeholders to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behaviour, and regional trends. The project aimed to support informed decision-making by transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

## 2. Data Understanding

The dataset was explored to understand its structure, identify important business attributes, and assess data quality. Key variables included sales, profit, customer information, product categories, regional performance, shipping details, and order history.

## 3. Data Preparation

The dataset was imported into Microsoft Power BI, where it was prepared for analysis. Data types were reviewed, fields were validated, and the data model was organised to support accurate reporting and interactive visualisations.

## 4. Data Modelling

Power BI was used to create calculated measures and KPIs using DAX. These measures enabled analysis of Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Average Order Value, Total Orders, and other business metrics required by decision-makers.

## 5. Data Visualisation and Analysis

Interactive dashboards were designed to present business performance from multiple perspectives, including executive KPIs, regional sales, product performance, customer purchasing behaviour, and profitability. Filters and slicers were incorporated to enable users to explore the data dynamically.

## 6. Evaluation

The completed dashboards were reviewed to verify that calculations, KPIs, and visualisations accurately represented the underlying dataset and effectively answered the project's business objectives.

## 7. Deployment

The completed dashboard, supporting report, dataset, and documentation were published on GitHub as a professional portfolio project demonstrating the complete business intelligence workflow.

# 📊 Dashboard Features

The GlobalMart Sales Analytics Dashboard provides an interactive business intelligence solution through multiple analytical views.

### Executive Dashboard
- Presents key performance indicators (KPIs) including Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, and Profit Margin.
- Provides a high-level overview of overall business performance.

### Regional Performance Dashboard
- Compares sales and profitability across different regions and states.
- Highlights geographical trends and identifies high-performing markets.

### Product Performance Dashboard
- Evaluates product categories and sub-categories based on sales, profit, and order volume.
- Identifies top-performing and underperforming products.

### Customer Analysis Dashboard
- Analyses customer purchasing behaviour and segment performance.
- Supports customer-focused business strategies through purchasing insights.

### Interactive Reporting
- Includes slicers and filters that allow users to analyse data dynamically by region, product category, customer segment, and other business dimensions.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

The analysis of GlobalMart's retail sales data revealed several important business insights:

- The **West** region generated the highest overall sales, demonstrating strong market performance compared to other regions.
- Significant differences exist between sales and profitability across regions, indicating that higher revenue does not always translate into higher profit.
- Certain product categories consistently outperform others, making them strong candidates for increased marketing and inventory investment.
- Customer purchasing behaviour varies across different segments, highlighting opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.
- Profit margins differ considerably between states, suggesting that pricing, discounts, and operational costs should be reviewed to improve profitability.

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the findings from the dashboard, the following recommendations are proposed:

- Increase investment in high-performing regions while identifying strategies to improve lower-performing markets.
- Review discount policies for products and regions with consistently low profit margins.
- Focus inventory planning on high-demand products to minimise stock shortages.
- Develop targeted marketing campaigns for valuable customer segments identified through the analysis.
- Continue monitoring business KPIs through interactive dashboards to support ongoing strategic decision-making.
